About

Hi, I'm your Wonderland Nanny

With years of hands-on experience caring for children of all ages, I created The Wonderland Nanny to offer families the kind of childcare I'd want for my own loved ones — warm, attentive, and wonderfully fun.

I believe every child deserves to feel safe, seen, and celebrated. Whether we're building blanket forts, practicing letters, or winding down for a nap, I bring patience, energy, and a gentle structure that helps little ones flourish.

Most of all, I want parents to feel completely at ease knowing their children are in caring, capable hands.
